UBC Museum of Anthropology
🎓
One of Vancouver's most impressive museums, featuring Indigenous art, history, and cultural artifacts from the Pacific Northwest and around the world.
✦ Our tip
On a clear day, combine the visit with a self-tour of the UBC Campus and a visit to Wreck Beach ;).
📍 Directions
2
~2.5 hours
Science World
🔬
Great for kids and adults alike, with hands-on exhibits covering science, engineering, nature, and technology.
✦ Our tip
Try to time your visit with a short film showing in their Science Theatre.
📍 Directions
3
~2 hours
Museum of Vancouver
🏛️
Learn about Vancouver's history, culture, and evolution through a variety of rotating exhibits.
✦ Our tip
Easy to pair with a quick visit to nearby Kits Point if the weather clears.
📍 Directions
4
~2 hours
Oakridge Park Mall
🛍️
Vancouver's newest luxury shopping destination, Oakridge Park is the newest mall and is partially open. Browse newly opened retailers, enjoy the modern design, and explore the Time Out Market featuring some of the city's top food vendors.
✦ Our tip
Come hungry—Time Out Market is the perfect place to sample a variety of local Vancouver favourites in one stop.
📍 Directions

Activities

What to Expect

1
Flexible
The Shed (Cypress Base Lodge)
🏔️
A casual alpine-style lodge at the base of Cypress Mountain that has a great view. Expect a laid-back ski lodge vibe even in summer.
✦ Our tip
Could be used as a two way stopover - grab coffee or a warm drink before heading to viewpoints or trails. Or, grab a bite to eat on your way down the mountain.
📍 Directions
3
Flexible
Cypress Lookout
🔭
One of the easiest viewpoints on the North Shore. From here you get sweeping views over Vancouver, the ocean, and surrounding islands.
✦ Our tip
Best at sunset. If clouds are low, skip it — visibility drops to almost nothing.
📍 Directions
4
15–30 min
Olympic Torch Photo Stop
🔥
Iconic photo stop from the 2010 Vancouver Winter Olympics. The torch site sits near the upper Cypress area and gives a mix of history and mountain backdrop.
✦ Our tip
Cypress Mountain also as a alpine-style lodge at the top. Check it out for coffee and snacks as well.
📍 Directions
5
1–2 hours
Eagle Coaster
🎢
A gravity-powered mountain coaster that runs through the forest from the top of Black Mountain. Ride a small cart on a fixed track after taking a scenic chairlift up, then control your own speed as you wind down over 1.7 km of twists, dips, and forest views.
✦ Our tip
Book ahead and choose an early time slot — lines build quickly. Go again if you can, since the experience changes depending on how fast you ride.
📍 Directions
6
Optional • Easy
Bowen Lookout
🌲
A short and easy forest walk leading to a quiet lookout with views toward Bowen Island and the Howe Sound area.
✦ Our tip
Great low-effort add-on if you’re already in the area — minimal elevation, well-marked, and quick to complete.
📍 Directions
7
Optional • Moderate
Hollyburn Peak
🫐
A forested hike with steady elevation gain leading to alpine-style views near the summit. In summer, you may find wild berries along the trail if conditions are right.
✦ Our tip
Bring water; the climb is steady and longer than you think.
📍 Directions
8
Optional • Moderate
St. Mark’s Summit
⛰️
One of the most popular hikes on the North Shore, leading to a wide open summit with sweeping views over Howe Sound.
✦ Our tip
Expect crowds on the platform, especially at sunset on a weekend. It’s popular for a reason — go early or late if you want space.
📍 Directions

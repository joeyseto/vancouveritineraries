A Day in
Whistler

The final stretch of the Sea to Sky — from Squamish north to Whistler — passes through some of BC's most dramatic scenery. A couple of unmissable pull-offs, then a world-class alpine village with as much (or as little) adventure as you want.

🚗 ~45 min from Squamish 📍 Hwy 99 North Full day recommended

~20 min north of Squamish · 25-40 min
Brandywine Falls
Brandywine Falls Provincial Park, Hwy 99
💧
A 70-metre waterfall that drops straight into a basalt canyon — one of the most dramatic waterfalls in BC and genuinely worth the 10-minute walk from the lot. The viewing platform puts you right at the edge of the gorge with the falls dead ahead.
✦ Our tip
Easy pull-off directly off the highway. It will take about 20 minutes and most people rank it as one of the best stops on the whole drive.
📍 Directions
2
The main event · Flexible
Whistler Village
Whistler, BC
🏔️
A world-class alpine village built at the base of two mountains. Pedestrian-only streets, great restaurants, boutique shopping, and mountain scenery from every angle. Whether you're here to explore on foot or do something bigger, this is a genuinely special place.
✦ Our tip
Walk the Village Stroll and around Lost Lake Park if you just want to take it easy. It's beautiful even without any added adventure.

Additional Activities

A
Optional · 1-4 hours
Whistler Gondola & Peak 2 Peak
🚠
Ride the gondola up from the village floor into full alpine terrain, then cross between Whistler and Blackcomb mountains on the iconic Peak 2 Peak — the longest unsupported gondola span in the world. Views stretch across glaciers, valleys, and layered peaks.
✦ Our tip
Skip on a cloudy day!
📍 Book tickets
B
Optional · 2–4 hours
Mountain Biking
Whistler Bike Park
🚵
Whistler Bike Park is considered one of the best lift-accessed mountain bike destinations on earth, with hundreds of trails spread across both mountains. Rentals, lessons, and guided options are all available right in the village.
✦ Our tip
Start with easier flow trails — terrain escalates quickly and it catches people off guard.
📍 Directions
D
Optional · 1–2 hours
Helicopter Tour
Whistler
🚁
A helicopter flight over glaciers, icefields, and rugged Coast Mountain ranges — one of the most dramatic ways to see the landscape you've been driving through. Tours depart directly from Whistler.
✦ Our tip
Morning flights have the clearest air and best visibility. This is the most expensive option but genuinely unlike anything else.
📍 Book a tour
E
Optional · 1–2 hours
Bungee Jumping
Whistler Bungee
🪂
One of Canada's most scenic bungee jumps off a canyon bridge over the Cheakamus River. Also offers zip lines and a suspension bridge if full bungee isn't your thing — the canyon itself is worth seeing either way.
✦ Our tip
Book ahead online — walk-up spots are limited, especially in summer. The gorge is spectacular even if you just do the suspension bridge.
